# easyPublisher
easypublisher-svg.svg

*A super lightweight publishing tool for people who want to write – not configure.*

**Write. Save. Publish.**  
easyPublisher turns your Markdown files into a working website — no setup, no database, no admin panel.  
Perfect for writers, bloggers, documentation, or personal notes.

→ No installation. No distractions. Just content.
